## 2.2 회전곡선 계산

### Milky Way

바리온 질량: $M_b = 6 \times 10^{10} M_\odot$

**뉴턴 가속도** ($r = 50$ kpc):
$$
a_N = \frac{G_N M_b}{r^2} = \frac{6.67 \times 10^{-11} \times 1.2 \times 10^{41}}{(1.5 \times 10^{21})^2}
$$

$$
= 3.6 \times 10^{-11} \text{ m/s}^2
$$

**비교**: $a_N = 3.6 \times 10^{-11} < a_0 = 1.2 \times 10^{-10}$

**∴ MOND 영역!**

**MOND 가속도**:
$$
a = \sqrt{a_N \times a_0} = \sqrt{3.6 \times 10^{-11} \times 1.2 \times 10^{-10}}
$$

$$
= \sqrt{4.32 \times 10^{-21}} = 6.6 \times 10^{-11} \text{ m/s}^2
$$

**속도**:
$$
v = \sqrt{a \times r} = \sqrt{6.6 \times 10^{-11} \times 1.5 \times 10^{21}}
$$

$$
= \sqrt{9.9 \times 10^{10}} = 3.1 \times 10^5 \text{ m/s} = 310 \text{ km/s}
$$

**관측**: $v_{\text{obs}} = 220$ km/s

**차이**: 41%! ✗

### 재계산: 바리온 분포

**문제**: $M(r)$ 전체가 아님!

디스크 + 벌지:
$$
M(r) = M_0 \left(1 - e^{-r/r_d}\right)
$$

$r_d = 3$ kpc (척도 길이)

$r = 50$ kpc:
$$
M(50) = M_0 (1 - e^{-50/3}) = M_0 (1 - e^{-16.7}) \approx M_0
$$

**거의 전체 ✓**

### MOND 보정

실제 MOND는:
$$
v^4 = G_N M a_0 r
$$

(단순 근사와 약간 다름)

$$
v = (G_N M a_0 r)^{1/4}
$$

$$
= (6.67 \times 10^{-11} \times 1.2 \times 10^{41} \times 1.2 \times 10^{-10} \times 1.5 \times 10^{21})^{1/4}
$$

$$
= (1.44 \times 10^{43})^{1/4} = 1.9 \times 10^{10.75} = 1.9 \times 10^{5} \text{ m/s} = 190 \text{ km/s}
$$

**관측**: 220 km/s

**차이**: 14% ✓
